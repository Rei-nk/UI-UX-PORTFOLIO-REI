01 — The Problem

Crypto Is Powerful. But Most Apps Make It Feel Terrifying.

Crypto wallets and payment apps are notorious for their complexity — raw wallet addresses, volatile market dashboards, and security warnings that read like legal disclaimers. For the average user, these aren't tools; they're obstacles.

02 — User Research

Mapping Fear Points Across User Journeys

I interviewed 12 potential users ranging from crypto beginners to intermediate traders. The top three pain points that emerged: fear of sending to the wrong address, confusion about transaction fees, and anxiety around price volatility during a payment flow.

03 — Design & Interfaces

Building Trust Through Visual Language

04 — Key Features

Designed for Confidence at Every Step

  • ▸Rate Lock Checkout: The exchange rate freezes for 60 seconds during payment confirmation — eliminating the fear of price slippage.
  • ▸Smart Address Book: QR scan, ENS name lookup, and saved contacts replace raw wallet addresses entirely.
  • ▸Fee Transparency: Network fees are shown in both crypto and fiat equivalents before any transaction is confirmed.
